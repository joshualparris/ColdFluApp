# Babies and young children — initial screening

## Retained as candidate sources

- RCH Kids Health Info: Bronchiolitis — direct Australian parent/carer scope covering breathing and feeding.
- RCH Kids Health Info: Dehydration in babies and children — direct Australian hydration and escalation scope.
- RCH Clinical Practice Guideline: Bronchiolitis — clinician context used only to understand applicability and hospital boundaries.
- NHMRC Bronchiolitis fact sheet — Australian transmission and childcare context.

## Excluded or deferred

- Translated and older PDF duplicates: deferred in favour of the current canonical RCH HTML page; translations will need a separate currency/accessibility check.
- RCH first-aid and whooping-cough pages: relevant to broader child safety but outside the initial bronchiolitis-focused screen.
- Search-result snippets: discovery only and never evidence.
- Hospital oxygen, fluid, sedative or transfer protocols: excluded from reader self-care instructions.
