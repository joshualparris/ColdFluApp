# Pregnancy and breastfeeding — evidence gaps

- Independent evidence-methods, Australian clinical and plain-language/accessibility reviews are not recorded.
- Systematic-review coverage for supportive care and medicine harms is incomplete.
- Antiviral treatment, exact timing and prescribing boundaries require a dedicated current Australian clinical search.
- Ingredient-level pregnancy and lactation evidence has not been independently checked against current product information and specialist references.
- Funding, conflicts, corrections and retractions have not been checked for any future study-level evidence.
- Escalation wording has not been reconciled across national and state maternity pathways.
- Evidence for comfort, symptom relief, illness duration, prevention, maternal harms, fetal harms and infant harms must remain separate.

These gaps block canonical integration and publication.
