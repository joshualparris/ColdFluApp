# Pregnancy and breastfeeding — screening

## Retained

The six exact sources in the extraction were retained because they identify their responsible organisation, population and Australian jurisdiction. The Immunisation Handbook is the primary current vaccination source. Healthdirect provides consumer pathway context. TGA defines how pregnancy categories must not be interpreted. MotherSafe provides specialist discovery context for later ingredient-level verification.

## Excluded or deferred

- Obsolete MotherSafe factsheets dated 2008 or 2010: excluded in favour of the July 2023 version.
- Search-result snippets and organisation homepages: excluded as evidence.
- Product pages and brand-level advice: excluded.
- Medicine tables copied into reader content: deferred pending ingredient-level source verification and Australian clinical review.
- Claims about exact antiviral timing or prescribing: deferred to a dedicated treatment search and clinical review.

No source is approved for canonical reader use by this screening decision.
