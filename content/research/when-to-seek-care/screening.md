# When to seek care — screening

## Retained

Healthdirect sources were retained as the national Australian consumer pathway. Existing population-specific hospital and condition sources are retained only within their exact scope.

## Excluded or deferred

- Search snippets and generic symptom lists: discovery only.
- Overseas emergency numbers and service pathways: excluded from the Australian overlay.
- Diagnosis-specific scoring systems: excluded.
- A single universal fever threshold: excluded because age and context matter.
- Wording that implies a person is safe because no listed sign is present: prohibited.
- Exact same-day versus next-day timing where sources do not explicitly support it: deferred.

No source is approved for canonical reader use by this screening decision.
