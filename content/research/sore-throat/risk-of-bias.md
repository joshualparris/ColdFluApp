# Sore throat — risk-of-bias and applicability notes

No study-level risk-of-bias assessment has been completed. NICE's evidence appraisal is used as a lead, not a substitute for extraction of the underlying trials. Key risks are indirect adult-to-child transfer, product/formulation differences, small subjective pain-score effects, selective reporting, older trials, and conflating a guideline recommendation with effectiveness evidence. Healthdirect and the Australian Government pages are authoritative for local orientation but are not controlled effectiveness studies.
