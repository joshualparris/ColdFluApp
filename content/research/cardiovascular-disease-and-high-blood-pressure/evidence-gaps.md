# Cardiovascular disease and high blood pressure — evidence gaps

- Independent evidence-methods, Australian cardiovascular clinical, pharmacist and plain-language/accessibility reviews are not recorded.
- Underlying studies for numerical infection, cardiac-event and vaccination-effect estimates have not been independently verified.
- Decongestant and NSAID harms require ingredient-, formulation-, dose- and condition-specific review.
- Interactions with antihypertensives, anticoagulants, antiplatelets, heart-failure medicines and antiarrhythmics are incomplete.
- Heart failure, coronary disease, arrhythmia, hypertension and stroke require clearer separate pathways.
- Exact escalation wording for chest pain, worsening breathlessness, fainting, confusion and fluid decompensation remains to be reconciled.
- Funding, conflicts, corrections and retractions remain to be checked for future study-level sources.

These gaps block canonical integration and publication.
