# Cardiovascular disease and high blood pressure — screening

## Retained

The sources were retained because they identify current Australian cardiovascular, consumer, regulator and immunisation boundaries. TGA label requirements corroborate the need for a professional check without turning a label example into a recommendation.

## Excluded or deferred

- Brand pages and individual consumer-medicine leaflets: deferred until a specific ingredient claim is proposed.
- Search snippets and promotional summaries: discovery only.
- Numerical heart-event and vaccine-effect estimates: deferred until their underlying studies are independently verified.
- Generic increased-fluid advice: excluded where it could conflict with a clinician-prescribed heart-failure plan.
- Medicine stop/start or dose-change instructions: excluded.

No source is approved for canonical reader use by this screening decision.
