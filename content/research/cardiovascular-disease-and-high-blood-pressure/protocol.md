# Cardiovascular disease and high blood pressure — research protocol

Status: structured pre-review research only. No canonical module or reader recommendation is created by this package.

## Scope

- Populations: people in Australia with cardiovascular disease, heart failure, high blood pressure, prior heart attack or stroke, or cardiovascular medicines.
- Core questions: respiratory-infection risk, prevention, fluid-plan conflicts, OTC medicine checks, duplicate ingredients, interactions and escalation.
- Exclusions: diagnosing cardiovascular disease, changing prescribed medicines, setting fluid or sodium limits, recommending decongestants or anti-inflammatory medicines, and estimating individual cardiovascular risk.

Cardiovascular disease is not one condition. Heart failure, coronary disease, arrhythmia, high blood pressure, stroke history and anticoagulation require separate medicine and fluid considerations.

## Outcomes

Keep prevention, infection severity, cardiac events, symptom comfort, medicine harms, fluid-related harms, illness duration and emergency escalation separate.

## Review gates

Independent evidence-methods, Australian cardiovascular clinical and plain-language/accessibility reviews are mandatory. Ingredient-level advice also requires current regulator/product information and pharmacist review.
