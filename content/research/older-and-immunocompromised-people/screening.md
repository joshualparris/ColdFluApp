# Older and immunocompromised people — screening

## Retained

The four exact sources were retained because they are current, identify the responsible Australian organisation, and directly cover population risk, care-seeking orientation or prevention. The Immunisation Handbook is the primary vaccination source; Healthdirect supplies consumer pathway context.

## Excluded or deferred

- Search snippets, campaign pages and organisation homepages: discovery only.
- Brand, product and dose tables: excluded from reader content.
- Old antiviral studies or pandemic-era treatment pages: deferred pending a focused current evidence search.
- COVID-19 treatment eligibility: outside this influenza-focused checkpoint and must not be transferred to influenza.
- Residential aged-care outbreak protocols: operational and clinical scope requiring a dedicated module or overlay.

No source is approved for canonical reader use by this screening decision.
