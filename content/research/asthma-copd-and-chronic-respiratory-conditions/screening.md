# Asthma, COPD and chronic respiratory conditions — screening

## Retained

The four sources were retained because they are current Australian national guidance or consumer pathways with direct relevance to infection-triggered respiratory deterioration and existing action plans.

## Excluded or deferred

- Search snippets and promotional winter checklists: discovery only.
- Prescriber algorithms, rescue-pack instructions and blank medicine fields: not general reader recommendations.
- Brand-specific inhaler content and exact first-aid dosing: excluded pending dedicated clinical and editorial review.
- Evidence from asthma transferred to COPD, or vice versa: prohibited without direct support.
- Other chronic respiratory diseases: deferred until diagnosis-specific sources are found.

No source is approved for canonical reader use by this screening decision.
