# Asthma, COPD and chronic respiratory conditions — evidence gaps

- Independent evidence-methods, Australian respiratory clinical and plain-language/accessibility reviews are not recorded.
- Antiviral, antibiotic, corticosteroid and inhaler-change benefits and harms have not been systematically reviewed.
- Exact emergency wording has not been reconciled with current condition-specific first-aid guidance.
- Vaccination outcomes must distinguish prevention of infection or severe disease from prevention of asthma or COPD exacerbations.
- Bronchiectasis, cystic fibrosis, interstitial lung disease, home oxygen and other chronic respiratory populations lack direct source coverage.
- Medicine interactions, comorbid cardiovascular disease and individual action-plan differences remain unresolved.
- Funding, conflicts, corrections and retractions remain to be checked for future study-level evidence.

These gaps block canonical integration and publication.
